The story in the book

Chapter 1

Molly had been staring out of her window for about an hour now. On her desk, lying between the copies of Nature,New Scientist, and all the other scientific journals her work had appeeared in, was a well thumbed copy of On the Road. It had been Molly's favorite book since college, and the longer she spent in these four wall the more she felt she needed to be free.

She had spent the last the last ten years in this room, sitting under a poster with an Oscar Wilde quite proclaiming that Work is the refuge of people who have nothing Although many considered her poineering work, unraveling the secrets of the llama DNA, to be an outstanding achievement, Molly, did think she had something better to do.
